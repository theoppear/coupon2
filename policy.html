The use of cookies

When a user visits a web site, the cookie file is recorded on the user's computer (if a user allows receipt of such files). If a user has already visited this web site, the cookie file is read from the computer. In addition to other purposes, we use cookies to facilitate the recording of visitor statistics. Such data helps us determine what type of information sent to customers can provide them with the most value. This data collection is conducted on a general basis and never affects the user's private information.

Third parties, including Google, display our company's advertisements on Internet web pages. Third parties, including Google, use cookies to display ads based on users' previous visits to our websites and their interests in web browsers. The user can prohibit Google from using cookies. To do so, you must go to Google's special page at this address: http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/
